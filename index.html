Owner Activation Agent

An outbound voice assistant that brings owners a fresh valuation of their home, listens to how they feel about their purchase, and walks them into their personal MoneyPark cockpit.

Hypothesis demo. This page lets you experience the agent in the browser by clicking the widget (inbound). In production it runs as an outbound call, which assumes a legal basis to contact owners without prior opt-in. It is the deliberate exception to the otherwise opt-in activation loop.

Try it

Click the widget in the corner and speak with Anna. She is set up with a sample owner profile so the conversation feels real.

Sample profile: Herr Thomas Müller, Bahnhofstrasse 12, 8001 Zürich, advised by Stefan Heitmann. The agent speaks German.

What the agent does

  1. 1Confirms identity, then introduces itself and discloses it is a virtual assistant.
  2. 2Delivers value first: the fresh estimate of the owner's property and how it has moved since purchase.
  3. 3Captures one data point: how satisfied the owner is with their purchase (CSAT).
  4. 4Activates the cockpit: sends a one-tap login link by SMS and offers to guide the first login live.
